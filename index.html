About Mhlongosa Logistics

Founded in 2021, we've grown from a single bakkie to a trusted fleet serving Cape Town and surrounding areas. We're a local family business that believes in honest work, fair prices, and treating your belongings like our own.

We work with individuals and companies across the Cape Peninsula. Every job gets the right vehicle – either a bakkie or a car – because we keep it simple and local.
