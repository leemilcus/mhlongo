About Mhlongosa Logistics

Founded in 2020, we've grown from a single bakkie to a trusted fleet serving Cape Town and surrounding areas. We're a local family business that believes in honest work, fair prices, and treating your belongings like our own.

We work with individuals (house moves, garden waste, single items) and companies (product deliveries, office relocations, waste removal) across the Cape Peninsula. Every job gets the right vehicle – either a bakkie or a car – because we keep it simple and local.
